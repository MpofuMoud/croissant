RECIPE

Ingredients
  • 550 grams of flour;
  • 35 grams of butter;
  • Pyatdesyat grams of starch;
  • 10 grams dry yeast
  • 150 milliliters of water;
  • 50 ml milk;
  • Seven teaspoons of sugar;
  • 325 grams of butter (to coat the dough);
  • Egg yolk (also for greasing test).

Method
First you need to sift the flour and mix with baking powder. Then add a dash of salt, sugar, yeast and mix well. Next, pour it into the milk and add the butter (softened) and water. Knead the dough, then cover it with cling film and leave until it increases about two times. Once the dough has risen, it is necessary to trample and put it in the fridge for half an hour.When the dough is ready, roll out into a thin layer, then cut out a circle and divide into eight pieces. Every part turn into the shape of a croissant. Leave our croissants so they came over, and then grease them with egg yolk and place on a baking tray, pre-greased with sunflower oil. The Oven should be already heated to 220 degrees, but the oven should be at a temperature of 190 degrees until Golden brown.
